The Recipe Tree

The Recipe Tree is an app that is used to gather and store family recipes. Family recipes are often considered heirlooms and are passed down from generation to generation. When you add a family recipe to the app, you give credit to the person who originated it. This process creates a family genealogy tree.

I performed both UX and UI duties on this project. I interviewed test subjects, created personas and a style guide, and incorporated all of this feedback into the functionality of the app. Using a Design Thinking process, I tested my wireframes for usability and A/B tested screens for user preference. I then took all of this feedback and developed the final screens.
